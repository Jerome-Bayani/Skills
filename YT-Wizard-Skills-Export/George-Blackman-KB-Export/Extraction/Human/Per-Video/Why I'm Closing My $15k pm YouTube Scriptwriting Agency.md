# Why I'm Closing My $15k pm YouTube Scriptwriting Agency

## What This Video Mainly Teaches
Business growth that removes the creative work you actually enjoy can quietly break the life the business was supposed to support.

## Strongest Takeaways
- Money-driven scaling can create a job you do not want.
- Creative operators need to notice when management work is crowding out the work they love.
- Long-term fit matters more than prestige.

## Useful Frameworks
- Lifestyle fit as a business filter
- Honest self-audit before scaling

## Useful Examples
- The agency became more managerial and less creative, which made the model feel wrong despite demand.

## Anti-Patterns / Warnings
- Scaling because the prestige sounds impressive
- Ignoring early signals that the work shape is wrong

## Best Phrases Worth Keeping
> "what is the life that I actually want to build for myself"

## Signal Level
- low

## Unique Contribution To The Channel Model
- Strongest business-philosophy context around why process design matters.
