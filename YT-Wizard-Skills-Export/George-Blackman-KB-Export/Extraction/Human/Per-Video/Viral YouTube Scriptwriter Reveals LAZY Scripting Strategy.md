# Viral YouTube Scriptwriter Reveals LAZY Scripting Strategy

## What This Video Mainly Teaches
Faster scriptwriting comes from being strategically lazy about the right things, not from lowering standards everywhere.

## Strongest Takeaways
- Choose scripting mode based on teleprompter access and speaking style.
- Separate research from writing.
- Templatize hooks and structures that already work.
- Limit revisions to avoid perfectionist drift.

## Useful Frameworks
- Strategic laziness
- Scriptwriting dial
- Two revision rule

## Useful Examples
- The video walks through scripting mode, research, templating, AI support, and revisions as one audit.

## Anti-Patterns / Warnings
- Full scripts without a teleprompter by default
- Endless revisions after the big problems are already solved

> "strategically lazy"
