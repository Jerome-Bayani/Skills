# The Claude Scriptwriting Hack Most YouTubers Miss

## What This Video Mainly Teaches
AI only improves if you force it to compare its draft with your final edited version and absorb the lessons.

## Strongest Takeaways
- Paste the final script back into Claude after editing.
- Generate a collaboration report that highlights the differences.
- Update the style guide and training doc after each script.

## Useful Frameworks
- AI self-review loop
- Style guide update loop

## Useful Examples
- Claude should learn from edits instead of being corrected outside the chat.

## Anti-Patterns / Warnings
- Editing AI output somewhere else and expecting future improvement

## Best Phrases Worth Keeping
> "force Claude to learn from its mistakes"
