# YouTubers With Low Retention Need To See This

## What This Video Mainly Teaches
Stakes should sit not only in the hook but also in each setup so the viewer keeps answering "why should I care?" with something concrete.

## Strongest Takeaways
- Add stakes to setups as well as hooks.
- Keep asking "why should I care?" until the cost of ignoring the next section is obvious.
- Specific worries hold attention better than generic promises.

## Useful Frameworks
- Hook stakes
- Setup stakes
- Repeated "why should I care?" test

## Useful Examples
- Careers advice, gym routines, and finance examples all show the same stakes pattern.

## Anti-Patterns / Warnings
- Assuming the viewer already feels the stakes just because they clicked
- Letting setups describe the next topic without emotional cost

## Best Phrases Worth Keeping
> "your viewers brains are continuously asking why"

## Signal Level
- high

## Unique Contribution To The Channel Model
- Best explanation of stakes beyond the hook.
