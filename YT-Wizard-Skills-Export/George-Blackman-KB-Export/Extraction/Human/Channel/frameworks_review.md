## Most Reusable Frameworks

- Setup -> tension -> payoff
- Target -> transformation -> stakes
- Hook / setup / payoff guardrails
- Human-AI hybrid segmented drafting
- Link -> curiosity -> promise

## Why These Matter

These frameworks are reusable because they are not topic-bound. They can be applied to educational, strategy, productivity, and many other YouTube formats as long as the creator needs structure, retention, and repeatability.
