# Worldview

- item: Scriptwriting is a system design problem more than a talent problem.
  confidence: high

- item: The best scripts emerge from repeatable rules, not blank-page heroics.
  confidence: high

- item: Retention data is useful because it can be translated into future writing behavior.
  confidence: high

- item: Constraints and templates are liberating when they reduce decision fatigue.
  confidence: high

- item: AI is valuable when it amplifies structured thinking, not when it replaces it.
  confidence: high

- item: The creator values freedom, clarity, and sustainable process over prestige for its own sake.
  confidence: medium
