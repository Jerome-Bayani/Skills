## Review Questions Template

- Who is this for?
- What transformation is promised?
- What are the stakes if the viewer ignores this?
- What is critical in the hook?
- What is only beneficial and should move later?
- Does the setup make the payoff worth waiting for?
- Is tension building need or just filling space?
- Is the payoff delayed correctly?
- Does each segment earn trust for the next one?
- Is the structure visible enough for the viewer?
- Is this too thin, too bloated, or correctly sized?

## Rebuild Threshold Template

Choose full rebuild when:
- the hook is solving the wrong problem
- the outline drifts without a clean spine
- the script sounds polished but generic
- the voice is wrong enough to hurt credibility
- the core promise is weak or muddy
